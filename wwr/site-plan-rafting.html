Overview

Purpose

Purpose of the site is to provide a place where turists from various places, families searching for relaxing and adventurous time together - to strenghten their bond, and nature & adventure lovers, can find best White water rafting sites in the country and easily schedule their trip, at affordable rates. Site will offer services of rafting in groups, families, and invidivuals, show tips of best places to stay, nearby restaurants and other sites to visit. Site will show importance of outdoors activities and preserving the envirnment.

Audience

Audience of the site are young men and women between age of 18 to 30, living and working in urban areas, or studying in college, that are planning a vacation or just a break from their stressing routine of work and studies and to have an exciting adventure or deeper contact with nature, in a relaxing environment. Other audience of the site are families with kids and in a stable financial situation, that are planning vacation trip or just tospend time together to remember and strenghten their bond.
The site audience can be accessing by their PC, work computer, smarphone or laptop
